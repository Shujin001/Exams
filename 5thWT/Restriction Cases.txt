Restriction Cases:
------------------

#1 Restriction on Values
a) Simple datatype: <xs:element name="age" type="xs:numeric" />
b) with facets:

simple-xsd
<xs:element name="age" type="xs:integer" />

facet-xsd
<xs:element name="age">
	<xs:simpleType>
		<xs:restriction base="xs:integer">
			<xs:minInclusive value="0" />
			<xs:maxInclusive value="120" />
		</xs:restriction>
	</xs:simpleType>
</xs:element>
